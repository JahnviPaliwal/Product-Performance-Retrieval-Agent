Acme Corporation was founded in 2015 and has grown to over 200 employees across five departments: Engineering, Marketing, Finance, HR, and Product.

The Engineering department is the largest team, responsible for building and maintaining all software products. They follow agile methodology with two-week sprints.

Acme's remote work policy allows all employees in Engineering, Marketing, and Product to work fully remotely. Finance and HR staff are expected to be on-site at least three days per week.

The company offers a comprehensive benefits package including health insurance, 401k matching up to 5%, 20 days of paid vacation, and an annual learning budget of $2,000 per employee.

Performance reviews are held twice a year in June and December. Salary adjustments and bonuses are tied to these reviews. The average company-wide bonus last year was 8% of annual salary.

The Product team works closely with Engineering and interfaces with all client-facing departments. The team uses Jira for project management and Figma for design work.

Acme's marketing strategy focuses primarily on content marketing and SEO. The team publishes three blog posts per week and manages social media channels across LinkedIn, Twitter, and YouTube.

The Finance department manages budgeting, payroll, and investor relations. Acme completed a Series B funding round of $25 million in 2022, led by Venture Capital Partners.

In 2023, Acme launched its flagship SaaS product "InsightPro", which uses AI to provide business intelligence dashboards. The product now has over 500 enterprise customers.

Employee satisfaction scores from the 2024 survey showed 87% of employees are satisfied or very satisfied with their work environment. The main areas for improvement noted were internal communication tools and career development pathways.
